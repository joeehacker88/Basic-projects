Gale Crater

Gale is a crater, and probable dry lake, in the northwestern part of the Aeolis quadrangle on Mars. It is 154 km wide and estimated to be about 3.5–3.8 billion years old. Peace Vallis, a nearby outflow channel, appears to have been carved by water. Curiosity rover landed there in 2012 and continues exploring.

Olympus Mons

Olympus Mons is a large shield volcano on Mars, standing over 21.9 km high. It's the tallest mountain in the Solar System. Known as "Nix Olympica" in the past, it was identified as a mountain before space probes confirmed it.

Sand Dunes

Sand dunes are common on Mars, some almost perfectly circular. Their shapes suggest wind-driven movement toward the south.
